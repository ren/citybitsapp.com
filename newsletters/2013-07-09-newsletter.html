Coffee, Coffee, Buzz, Buzz!

This month found us in Amherst, MA. We've long heard about the coffee shops in and around the 'herst (yeah, we might have just made that up). In brief, our first takes:

We coffee'ed at Rao's (Yelp). Besides a website that won't load, Rao's makes an excellent latte and is just a beautiful place to go. Cold brew aficionados note that this is the real deal. Mocha lovers, be sure to order a little of that homemade whipped cream on top.

After a nice walk, in the name of research, we re-coffee'ed (how is that for making a made up word longer?) at Amherst Coffee (http://highercup.com), which is a place I could have spent the day and the evening. Excellent espresso drinks, with a full bar that opens at 3pm. We didn't have time for a drink, but the always "à point" Mel & Whit gave them a hearty stamp of approval.

If Amherst starts to feel a little small, head out of town to The Lady Killigrew Cafe (photos) in Montague, MA. The Lady Killigrew is a beauty overlooking the water. Adjoining the Montague Book Mill, there is a lot to love. Their restaurant, The Night Kitchen, receives rave reviews.

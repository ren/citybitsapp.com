1,000 Words

Readable in the time it takes to taxi to your gate, CityBits is the city guide that clocks in at 1K words or less. Written for travelers with a day or two, CityBits can serve as a foundation for a longer trip.

CityBits aims to provide choices that are not only wonderful experiences, but are integrated into the health of their local communities.

Curated

Superb recommendations are the reason we exist. We pull from all your favorite sources (FourSquare, Yelp, Zagat, local blogs, local people), then review in person.

All that said, our most important source is you, we want to know about the places that make your heart tick.
